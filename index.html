Descriptions

Red Light

When you see a red light on the traffic controller, you are supposed to come to a complete stop. Failure to do so could result in serious injury or even death.


Yellow Light

When you see a yellow light, you should prepare to slow down as the red light will be shown within a few seconds of this color. On the other hand, if you are almost through the light when it turns yellow you should proceed through the light as this is a warning for the coming red light.


Green Light

When you see the green light, you are supposed to drive through the intersection safely. Although, it is critical that you pay attention to your surroundings as other drivers can cause injury or death even if your light is green. It is recommended to do this with all colors of the light as well.
